What are your limits?

If you're wondering about our service limits and how they work, you're in the right place.

What are your domain limits?

There's none! All plans provide use of unlimited domains.

What are your mailbox limits?

This depends on your plan. We have three simple plans:

Solo - 1 Mailbox
Pro - 10 Mailboxes
Agency - Unlimited Mailboxes

What are your sending limits?

Our sending limit is simple: 300 outgoing emails per hour, per mailbox/use case.

You may not use multiple mailboxes for the same purpose to circumvent this limit for the same use case. However, you can (and should!) use separate mailboxes for each separate purpose/use case.

ℹ️ If you need to send more than 300 transactional emails per hour, please let us know - we can increase this limit on a case-by-case basis. This is because our outgoing email provider enforces limits to prevent abuse by spammers and maintain high levels of deliverability.

For a more detailed explanation, please see below:

Allowed ✅
form@example.com - Contact Form Notifications
website@example.com - WordPress Website Notifications
billing@example.com - Billing Notifications
form@examplepetstore.com - Gener
billing@examplepetstore.com - 
website@examplepetstore.com

In this example, our customer has separated their mailboxes for different purposes for example.com and examplepetstore.com, so they can send up to 300 outgoing emails/hour for each different purpose/use case.

ℹ️ Whilst they could use just one mailbox per domain or for multiple use cases, they have created separate mailboxes for each purpose to avoid reaching the 300/hour limit.

Not allowed ❌

billing@example.com - Used for billing at example.com
billing2@example.com - Used to exceed the 300/h limit
billing3@example.com - Used to exceed the 300/h limit

In this example, our customer is using 3 different mailboxes for the same purpose/use case, in order to circumvent the send limit. This is not allowed, and is considered abuse of our service.

If you are reaching need to send more than 300 transactional emails per hour for your use case, please get in touch to discuss increasing your limits.
