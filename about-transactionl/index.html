About Transactionl

What is Transactionl?

Transactionl is a transactional e-mail service.

You provide the domain, and we provide transactional email for your applications. We provide a strong focus on outbound e-mail delivery, with a focus on reliable inbox placement and deliverability.

We understand that anyone can run an inbound mail server, but getting your e-mail delivered to Gmail, Hotmail, Verizon, AT&T, and Yahoo from one IP address is almost a fantasy. Transactionl makes this easy - at a very low cost.

Who is behind Transactionl?

Transactionl is a sister company of Webfluencer, a web design and development business.

After creating many websites for small to medium businesses, it was clear that email provided by web hosts didn't deliver on their promises, and was often an after-thought, with little to no enforcement of outgoing spam on their networks. I, and my customers, didn't want excuses on why email didn't always reach recipients, so we switched to MXRoute for outgoing application emails for our clients. After a careful transition, we were very happy with MXRoute's services and decided to become a reseller of their services.

How can I trust a reseller?

After extensive testing with MXRoute with our own services and clients, we're really happy with the services provided by them. We believe in their no-nonsense approach to email spammers, as their network always maintains high-reputation IP addresses, which leads to top deliverability rates in the inbox, and not the spam folder.
