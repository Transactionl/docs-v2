Who is Transactionl aimed towards?

Transactionl is aimed towards small/medium businesses that need transactional email across multiple websites and applications, with top deliverability at a low cost.

Example use-cases

Some examples of customers using Transactionl are:

  • WordPress, Drupal & Joomla: Our customers use Transactionl with popular content management systems. Some examples are password resets, post notifications, comment notifications, and WordPress update notifications.
  • E-commerce websites: Our customers use Transactionl with applications such as Shopify and WooCommerce. Some examples are order confirmations, shipping notifications, and delivery updates.
  • Agencies: Many of our customers are agencies that manage websites and applications on behalf of multiple clients. Some example use-cases are CRMs such as Salesforce and Zoho CRM, and e-commerce websites using WooCommerce and Shopify.
