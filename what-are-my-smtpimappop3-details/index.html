What are my SMTP/IMAP/POP3 details?

In most cases, all you require is SMTP to use Transactionl with your application.

Email Address: The email address you created in DirectAdmin (e.g. user@example.com)
Password: The password you set for the mailbox in DirectAdmin.
Server/Hostname: everest.mxrouting.net
SMTP SSL Port: 465 (Recommended)
SMTP Non-SSL/STARTTLS: 25, 587, 2525 (Not recommended due to security)

If you'd like to use your own email client instead of webmail to read/delete bounce emails, you can use the following protocols/ports:

IMAP SSL: 993
IMAP Non-SSL: 143
POP3 SSL: 995
POP3 Non-SSL: 110
